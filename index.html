About Jason

A journey of innovation, education, and bridging cultures across continents

Hi, I'm Jason Guowei You — a lifelong learner, entrepreneur, and bridge-builder between cultures, generations, and ideas.

Born in China and rooted in the San Francisco Bay Area, I transferred from De Anza College to UC Berkeley, where I studied Environmental Economics and Policy alongside Asian Diaspora Studies. My journey has always been guided by a deep desire to connect people and ideas across boundaries — whether through education, storytelling, or technology.

I'm the founder and CEO of InterBridge, an AI-powered education platform that empowers international and ESL students to thrive socially and professionally in the English-speaking world. We're proud to be part of Bay Area Founders Club Batch 25, and we're building the next generation of global talent through scenario-based learning, mentorship, and digital storytelling.

Before starting InterBridge, I worked at Zhencheng Capital and ZhenFund, where I learned firsthand how to spot visionary founders and scalable solutions. That experience solidified my belief: the future belongs to those who act boldly and build for others.

I'm a voracious reader. Two books that recently shaped me are What It Takes: Lessons in the Pursuit of Excellence by Stephen Schwarzman and The Ride of a Lifetime by Robert Iger. Both reaffirmed the power of resilience, empathy, and long-term vision — qualities I strive to embody in my work and leadership.

In the long run, I hope to give back to the world through social entrepreneurship, public service, and ethical AI development. Whether it's designing solutions for marginalized students, participating in political reform, or building human-centric technologies, I want my career to be in service of a more equitable and inspired future.

If this resonates with you, I'd love to connect.
